Winter Training Planner

Winter training is separate from matches: Brownedge only, Monday to Friday, with 18:00, 19:00, and 20:00 one-hour slots. Each slot has 3 training areas and teams use their shared training area requirement and preferred training time.

Summer Training Planner

Summer training uses the same weekdays and times as winter training, but it runs across summer-enabled venues using the area capacity set on each venue.
